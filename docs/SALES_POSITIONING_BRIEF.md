# YourBarber Sales Positioning Brief

This is the sales message spine for the public website, demo flow, and future copy work.

## Core Positioning

YourBarber is the walk-in queue system that remembers every client.

## Shortest Pitch

One QR code. One live queue. Every client remembered.

## Commercial Pitch

YourBarber helps walk-in barbershops run a sharper queue, remember every client's haircut, and bring regulars back from one QR code on the wall.

## What It Is

A live walk-in queue, Cut Passport, and reminder system for independent barbershops.

## Who It Is For

- Independent barbershops
- Walk-in shops
- Turkish barbers
- Owner-led shops
- Modern grooming studios

The product should feel practical, premium, and built for real barbershops rather than corporate salons.

## The Problems It Solves

Do not lead with technology. Lead with the barber's real-world pain:

- Customers asking how long the wait is
- Barbers juggling who is next
- Regulars expecting the barber to remember their cut
- Customers disappearing and coming back confused about their place
- Haircut notes living in somebody's head
- Follow-up and reminders rarely happening properly

## The Three-Part Story

The queue gets people in the chair.

The Cut Passport keeps them coming back.

The reminder loop brings regulars back before they drift.

This is the main commercial structure.

## Hero Message

Headline:

The walk-in queue system that remembers every client.

Subheading:

Customers scan your QR code, join the queue, and choose today's cut from their phone. Your barbers see who's waiting, what they want, and what they had last time.

## Strong Positioning Line

Built for walk-ins, not just bookings.

## Cut Passport Message

Every regular gets a Cut Passport.

It stores:

- Haircut photos
- Grades
- Notes
- Visit history
- Preferences
- Next-visit timing

So when someone says "same as last time", the barber can actually see what that means.

## Queue Message

A live queue the whole shop can trust.

Customers:

- Scan the wall QR
- Join from their own phone
- See where they stand
- Get a text when they are next

Result:

- Calmer shop
- Clearer lobby
- Fewer walkouts
- Less "who's next?" drama

## Reminder Message

Bring regulars back before they drift.

When someone has not been in for a few weeks, YourBarber sends a polite reminder at the right time.

Result:

- More repeat visits
- Filled quiet mornings
- Less manual follow-up

## Demo Story

The demo should prove this exact journey:

1. Customer scans the wall QR
2. Customer joins the live queue
3. Barber sees them instantly
4. Barber opens the Cut Passport
5. Owner sees the whole shop in the dashboard
6. Reminder loop brings the client back later

## Tone Rules

Avoid:

- database
- API
- module
- interface
- technical stack talk
- "not a booking app" as the hero message

Prefer:

- memory
- history
- queue
- tools
- regulars
- sharper shop
- built for walk-ins
- more professional experience

## The Feeling We Want

The product should feel:

- Practical
- Premium
- Barber-first
- Commercially clear
- Less like software
- More like a secret weapon for running the shop better
